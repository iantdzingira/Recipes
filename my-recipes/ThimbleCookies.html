Thimble Cookies

These thimble cookies are another old favorite of my mother's. A neighbor was making these when I was a child and I never got enough of them.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 1 egg yolk, beaten
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 cup finely chopped walnuts
  • 1 cup raspberry jam, or as needed

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 2

Cream butter and sugar; add yolk and lemon juice and beat well. Stir in flour until well blended.

Step 3

Form into 1-inch balls, dip into beaten egg white then into chopped nuts. Place on a greased cookie sheet, make a deep indentation in the center of each cookie.

Step 4

Bake for 5 minutes, then quickly indent centers again, continue baking for 10 minutes.

Step 5

Fill center of each hot cookie with raspberry jam and serve.
